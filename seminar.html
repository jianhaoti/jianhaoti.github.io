Geometry and Analysis Student Seminar

Date Speaker Abstract
1/18 Paul Tee Second and Third Existence Theorems, Regularity of Weak Solutions: In this talk we will see how our previous work and the Fredholm Alternative combine to give two more existence theorems. We then show (time permitting) that for f in H^m, a weak solution u to the equation Lu=f will belong to H^{m+2}.
